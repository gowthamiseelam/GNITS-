KALINGA



Kalinga Movie:A rural drama with impressive direction and visually stunning moments

  Kalinga follows Linga (Dhruva Vaayu), an orphan from Kalinga village in Telangana. Though carefree and fond of drinking, Ling is deeply in love with Paddu (Pragya Nayan). His life turns upside down when Bali (Sanjay), the troublemaking brother of the village head (Aadukalam Naren), sets his sights on Paddu, creating a rift between the couple. To win Paddu's hand, Linga must clear his family’s debts, all while fending off Bali's advances
